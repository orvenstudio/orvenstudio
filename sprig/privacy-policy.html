Sprig ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA). This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights as a user.

Data Retention & Deletion

On-device data is immediately deleted when you uninstall the App. Analytics data collected via Firebase is retained according to Google's default retention period (14 months). Cloud-synced data can be removed by disconnecting your account or using the delete function within the App.

Sprig is not directed at children under 14. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that a child's data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
